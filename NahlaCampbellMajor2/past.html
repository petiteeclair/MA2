2019- Rose Quartz from Steven Universe

I touched on this before, but this was for a Stevonnie cosplay. Stevonnie is the fusion of Steven, the main character, and Connie, his best friend and romantic interest. As Steven weilds Rose's shield since he inherited her gem, Connie weilds Rose's sword. Together, as Stevonnie, the two are able corrupted gems and adversaries alike with their combined strengths.

For this project, I used simple materials because I'm not very experienced. I used foam boards that I cut with an exacto knife for the blade and regular foam for the hilt. I painted the sword and scabbard in various shades of pink which I had to repaint several times to get the shades I wanted. I used a wooden dowel that I painted green with light green thorns for the grip. I bought some pink and red roses with leaves to fill in the white gaps with hot glue and to burn my fingers, apparently. I also included a sheer, white lace that I used for a previous Halloween costume.

My favorite part of this project is actually the hilt. I know it isn't a carbon copy of Rose's sword in the show, but that's what I love about it. It's got a little of my own influences and interpretation. I especially love the leaves along the loop guard. The truth is that it was made of foam from the hilt and I needed some way to cover it up, so I thought "leaves should work". And it did!

The shield was a little tricky. Even now, it's still somewhat incomplete. For this, I used a bowl from party city and spray painted it pink with a gold shimmer. Then I took a lighter pink paint and painted a spiral of torns and light green along the base. And then I used a spare piece of stock paper to make the center design and hot glued it.

I tried to make some sort of backpack or drawstring bag inside the shield and glue the string from the bag to the insides. It sort of works, as long as I distribute the weight equally on my arm. But I may need to consider making a new shield of lighter material and not a plexiglass bowl from Party City...

2020- Connie Maheswaren from Steven Universe

I was extremely proud of this and I still am! I made this at the start of quarentine when I foolishly believed that we'd only have to quarentine for a couple of months or so. At the time, my friends and I were planning on going to Empire City Con in North Carolina and I planned on cosplaying as Connie in her adorable space camp outfit from the movie. The convention was supposed to be in November just before Thanksgiving, but...

I used pretty much the same materials in this project as I did with Project Rose. I had to do something a little more geometric to the get that angular look. It was a little easier than the curved edges in Rose's.

I really like the touches with the flowers and star stickers and pearls. I'm really happy with the pearl design I put on the scabbard because I think it serves to show where Connie learned everything she knows about swordsmanship: Pearl! To be completely honest, I added the purple and blue flowers to fill in some blank or empty spaces on the sword and to highlight the difference between the pale and saturated yellows of the scabbard.
