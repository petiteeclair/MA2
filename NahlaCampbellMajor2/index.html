Let's Build Some Cosplay Weapons!

I love cosplay. It's an artform that I think is so fun and allows everyone of any age, gender, body type, or race to make something that represents a show, movie, book, or game that they really enjoy and be able to show off that love at conventions for hundreds of people. So, while I'm not a great at sewing, I enjoy trying to make weapons for my favorite characters. It's a fun activity to do every now and then to help me destress and allow me get lost in creating things. I know that my creations aren't on a professional level, but just knowing that I put in the time and effort to making something that I'm proud of is more than enough for me. The first weapon that I made (featured below) was Rose's sword and scabbard from popular, Cartoon Network show, Steven Universe for a Stevonnie Halloween cosplay in 2019. Unfortunately, I'd gotten sick the day of and wasn't able get dressed, but I could always wear it to a convention! Or so I thought... 2020 came and kind of ruined any plans to do anything, but I still tried to find time to create something new and come up with a new idea that I'm really excited to share!
